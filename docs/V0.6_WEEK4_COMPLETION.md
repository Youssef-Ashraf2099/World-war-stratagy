### Key Features

1. **Neighbor Detection** ✅
   - Identifies neighboring nations via province ownership
   - Excludes parent nation and other factions
   - Deterministic and reproducible

2. **Intervention Decision** ✅
   - 50% random chance to intervene (seeded)
   - Considers diplomatic relations (reputation > -10)
   - Faction selection (currently random)

3. **Military Aid** ✅
   - Transfers 1-3 armies from intervener to faction
   - Uses deterministic shuffle
   - Preserves army stats

4. **Resource Aid** ✅
   - Provides: food (100), iron (50), oil (25), rare_earths (10), water (75)
   - Distributed to faction provinces
   - Boosts faction economy

5. **Reputation System** ✅
   - Framework for success (+15) / failure (-10, refugees)
   - Integrated with DiplomaticRelation

6. **Pipeline Integration** ✅
   - Added Phase 15 (after FactionCivilWarPhase)
   - Executes every tick
   - No performance impact
